Information About me

Since a young age, I have always been involved in the tech world. It all began when I started writing my own scripts for an MMORPG, called Ultimia Online. I started playing Ultimia Online when I was 8 years old and began creating in-game scripts to train my characters while I was away at school. As basic as this was, it was enough to motivate me to always be the best at every game that I played and sparked an interest for learning how games were created.

Towards the end of 2015, I pursued my interest in coding by joining the Coding Dojo Web Development Bootcamp. Here I gained helpful knowledge that could be applied to different technologies and advanced skill sets that are necessary to be a self-sufficient, full-stack web developer by learning Ruby on Rails, Python, and Javascript. While I am always striving to improve my knowledge and skills, my main focus is in ReactJS and React Native. I place an emphasis on clean code, nice software design, and best practices.
